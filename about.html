About Us

Our Mission and Vision

At the Mother Teresa Mission Centre, our mission is to bring hope and love to the world by serving the less fortunate and working towards a better tomorrow. We envision a community where everyone has access to basic needs and opportunities for growth.

History of the Centre

Founded in 2000, the Mother Teresa Mission Centre has been dedicated to providing support and care to those in need. Over the years, we have expanded our services to include healthcare, education, feeding programs, and shelter assistance.

Our journey began with a small group of volunteers who shared a common goal: to make a difference in the lives of the less fortunate. Today, we are proud to serve thousands of individuals and families in our community.
